Membership


MOVES is an International Non-Governmental Organisation for schools in sports and exercise, primarily on the Vocational Education Training level. This network focuses on improving the quality of education by exchanging students, teachers and information in an international context

MOVES is always interested in expanding its network. Therefore we are looking for suitable new partners who share our mission and vision.

We hope visiting our website has made you enthusiastic. If so you can contact us by using the email addresses on the contact page.
