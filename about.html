About Us


Vision and mission

Mission (23-11-2017)

MOVES is an European Non-Governmental Organisation for schools in sports and exercise, primarily on the Vocational Education Training level. This network focuses on improving the quality of education by providing an international experience. MOVES includes all parties in the educational system in its activities; heads of schools, teachers, students and employers


Vision (23-11-2017)

It is the ambition of MOVES to share information between the schools of the network on an international level
By sharing knowledge, skills and competencies
By encouraging exchange possibilities of teachers & students
By sharing information about the educational systems in different countries
By setting up a communication system between the different members of the network
By sharing teaching methods and national work placement networks
By organising an annual conference for heads of schools, teachers, students & employers
